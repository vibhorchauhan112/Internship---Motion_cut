Thai Green Curry

A fragrant and spicy Thai curry with coconut milk, green chilies, and vegetables.

  • Green curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken or tofu
  • Eggplant, bell pepper
  • Thai basil
  1. Heat oil and sauté green curry paste for 2 minutes.
  2. Add chicken or tofu and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  5. Stir in Thai basil and cook for 2 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with steamed jasmine rice.

Greek Moussaka

A traditional Greek casserole layered with eggplant, spiced meat, and creamy béchamel.

  • Eggplants
  • Minced lamb or beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, garlic
  • Béchamel sauce
  1. Slice and roast eggplants until soft.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic, add minced meat and tomato sauce. Cook thoroughly.
  3. Layer eggplant and meat sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Top with béchamel sauce and bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  5. Let it cool slightly before serving.

Japanese Sushi Rolls

Delicate rolls of vinegared rice, seaweed, and assorted fillings like fish and vegetables.

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Raw fish or vegetables
  • Rice vinegar
  • Soy sauce, wasabi
  1. Cook rice and mix with rice vinegar.
  2. Place nori on bamboo mat, spread rice evenly.
  3. Add sliced fish or veggies and roll tightly.
  4. Cut into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce and wasabi.

Mexican Tacos

Crispy or soft tortillas filled with seasoned meat, veggies, and tangy toppings.

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or beans
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Avocado, sour cream
  1. Cook meat with taco seasoning until browned.
  2. Warm tortillas and layer with meat and toppings.
  3. Add cheese, avocado, and sour cream.
  4. Serve immediately with salsa or lime wedges.

French Ratatouille

A hearty Provençal stew made with tomatoes, zucchini, and bell peppers.

  • Zucchini
  • Eggplant
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs de Provence
  1. Chop all vegetables uniformly.
  2. Sauté each vegetable separately until slightly soft.
  3. Layer in a pot and season with herbs and salt.
  4. Simmer for 25–30 minutes until tender.
  5. Serve hot with bread or rice.

American Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes served with butter, syrup, or fruits for a classic breakfast.

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar and salt
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk and eggs until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter into small circles.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side.
  5. Serve warm with maple syrup and butter.
